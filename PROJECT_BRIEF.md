# Project Brief
## Classifying Key Skills in the Video Games Industry

### Project Description
This project involves developing an interactive dashboard based on data scraped from games industry-specific job listing websites. This dashboard will summarise the UK games industry job market, providing a breakdown of key skills, experience, and software that appear in job descriptions.

**Purpose**: Support strategies to develop the local games industry through the development and delivery of workshops for young people, targeting the most requested skills, experience, and software.

### Stakeholders
- Project Director
- Project Team Members
- Leicester Games Network Members
- School and College Teachers

### Requirements
1. **Data Sources**: Draw from multiple job listing websites specialising in the video games industry
2. **Keyword Analysis**: Summary of keywords including skills, experience, and software
3. **Trend Tracking**: Show how demand changes over time
4. **Daily Updates**: Data must be updateable daily, suited for non-technical users
5. **Visualisations**: Multiple types (heatmaps, histograms, pie charts, time series)
6. **Interactivity**: Data must be searchable and filterable, with visualisations updating accordingly
7. **Client-Provided Keywords**: Keywords, software etc. will be provided by the client at project start

### Context
The UK is one of the largest games industries in the world, with London being the most densely populated area for employment. In the Midlands, Nottingham and Leamington Spa have the most studios, however **Leicestershire has very few studios** despite having three universities and many schools and colleges providing relevant courses.

This dashboard will help identify up-to-date requirements listed on games industry job descriptions, forming part of a strategy to engage young people with the prospect of joining the games industry.

### Geographic Focus
- **Primary**: UK games industry
- **Regional**: Leicestershire/Leicester area
- **Comparison areas**: London, Nottingham, Leamington Spa
